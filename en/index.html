How do I download the app on Android?

  • Browse the mobile options and go to "Settings".
  • Click on "Advanced Settings" (this varies depending on your Smartphone).
  • Activate "Unknown Sources"
  • Confirm your choice by clicking on "OK".
  • Open the internet browser and go to "www.meridianbet.be"
  • You will see the screen "Download our Android Application". Click on "Android"
  • If you do not see this screen: go to "Menu" > bottom > "Mobile applications" > bottom > "Download Android"
  • Click on "Download"
  • Browse your downloads.
  • Click on "app-be-release.apk"
  • Install the Meridianbet.be app on your mobile.
  • Congratulations, the app is now successfully installed on your mobile. You can now register or log in.
